#summary ReasonerAdapter component specification for Menta 0.2.

= ReasonerAdapter. =
Reasoner adapter is the interface and translator to the probabilistic [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Semantic_reasoner reasoner] statements.
Main purpose is to provide the adapter and translation from the [http://code.google.com/p/menta/wiki/Knowledge Knowledge] classes and individuals into the Reasoner objects.
